Develop, maintain, and optimise Green-Tech websites and digital platforms.

Maintain documentation, version control, and CI/CD workflows.

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, IT, or equivalent practical experience.
Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern frameworks (React, Next.js, Vue, Angular).
Experience with back-end development (Node.js, PHP, Python, or similar).
Strong knowledge of databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB) and API integrations.
Experience with version control (Git) and continuous deployment workflows.
Understanding of responsive design, cross-browser compatibility, and web security practices.
Strong problem-solving ability and attention to detail.
Excellent communication skills and ability to work independently.
Fluent in Vietnamese and English to support cross-border tourism and sustainability projects.
