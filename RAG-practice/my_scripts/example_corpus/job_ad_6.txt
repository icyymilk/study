About MyConnect

MyConnect helps Australians moving house get their utilities connected – fast, easily, and reliably. We’re growing quickly, and we’re looking for a Developer Programmer (with a strong DevOps focus) to help us scale our cloud infrastructure, accelerate our deployments, and strengthen the resilience and efficiency of our systems.

This is a hands-on role where you’ll contribute across programming, automation, and cloud engineering, shaping how we design, build, deploy, and operate everything we deliver.

Key Responsibilities

Analyse user needs, translate requirements into technical specifications, and design, develop, and maintain program code for applications and systems.
Build, test, debug, and document software applications to ensure they perform to specification and meet business and technical requirements.
Design and maintain secure, reliable AWS infrastructure using Infrastructure-as-Code (CloudFormation, ECS, RDS, S3, IAM, etc.).
Develop and maintain CI/CD pipelines using Bitbucket Pipelines and AWS CodePipeline, enabling fast and reliable software delivery.
Automate deployment, provisioning, scaling, monitoring, and backup tasks to improve efficiency and reduce operational overhead.
Identify deficiencies in existing applications, systems, and processes, and recommend improvements or upgrades.
Collaborate with engineers on architecture migration from monolith to microservices, deployment strategies, monitoring, and incident response.
Write and maintain technical documentation, end-user guides, and operational procedures to support software and infrastructure.
Monitor system and application performance using CloudWatch and related tools, diagnosing and resolving errors, bottlenecks, and faults.
Contribute to security, compliance, and audit readiness by applying best practices (ISO 27001 exposure desirable).
Participate in full-stack development where needed:
Frontend: Next.js, React, Blazor.
Backend/API: Node.js, TypeScript, C#/.NET Core.
Required Skills & Experience

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience).
3+ years’ experience in software development with exposure to DevOps or cloud engineering.
Strong programming ability across multiple languages (Node.js, TypeScript, C#/.NET Core, Python, Bash).
Proficiency in AWS services and infrastructure-as-code (CloudFormation).
Hands-on experience with CI/CD pipelines and containerised deployment workflows.
Strong debugging, diagnostic, and problem-solving skills.
Familiarity with frontend frameworks and backend API development.
Nice to Have

Experience with AWS Fargate, Lambda, or serverless tooling.
Exposure to Datadog or similar observability stacks.
Familiarity with ISO 27001 or equivalent compliance frameworks.
Experience in growth-stage or scaling environments.
